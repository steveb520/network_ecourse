# Module 5: Crafting Your Elevator Pitch

## Lesson 5.1: What Is an Elevator Pitch?
An elevator pitch is a concise explanation of who you help, what problem you solve, and the value you provide.

## Lesson 5.2: Building Your Pitch
Effective pitches follow a simple structure: problem, solution, next step.

## Lesson 5.3: Tailoring Your Pitch
Adapting language and tone increases relevance and engagement.

## Exercise
Write and practice three versions of your pitch.
